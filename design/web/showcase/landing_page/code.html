The Narrative

Precision isn't just a measurement; it's a philosophy. We celebrate the era when hardware was sculpted, and every screw served a purpose.

This collection is born from a lifelong obsession with industrial design. Each device in the Atelier has been sourced, cleaned, and restored to its original glory. From the pebble-like curves of the Bondi Blue iMac to the rigid, geometric perfection of the NeXTcube.
